Building The Network

Lifetime Networks is an organization that helps people with disabilities build a circle of supportive friends that extends beyond their immediate families. In 2010, Life Networks merged with the charitable organization Citizen Advocacy of Ottawa, for whom I previously designed various forms of paper media and logos. Life Networks asked me to create an infographic for them called "Building The Network", to explain their process of helping new members develop a supportive circle of friends.
